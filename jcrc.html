JCRC

30th Junior Common Room Committee

The Junior Common Room Committee (JCRC) is the student representative body of Temasek Hall, elected annually by the residents of the Hall. It lays down the vision and provides a strategic planning and oversight for the Academic Year.

They are led by a President and Vice-President, and assisted by an Honorary General Secretary, as well as 9 Directors: Finance, Sports (Internal & External), Hall Affairs, Social Affairs, Cultural, Operations, External Relations and Special Projects.
